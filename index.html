    Hybrid pipeline & efficiency

      MobileNet v3 · small

    2. 02

      ca-LIF spiking head

      A leaky integrate-and-fire layer whose leak β and threshold ϑ adapt to each image.

    3. 03

      Breed by spike count

      Evidence accumulates over time; the class that spikes most, and soonest, wins.

    Illumination-Invariant Certified Robustness

    A construction and a fundamental limit. A rank front-end beneath a 1-Lipschitz network yields predictions exactly invariant to every monotonic illumination change, then proves the sharp price that invariance has to pay.
