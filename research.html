02 The standard

A result is only as strong as the experiment designed to break it.

  1. 01

    Pre-register the question

    Decide what evidence would count before the experiment knows the answer.

  2. 02

    Build the strongest baseline

    Compare against dense controls with equal capacity, tuning and reporting.

  3. 03

    Publish the uncertainty
