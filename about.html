02 The studio

One person, one standard.

Sonne Systems is intentionally compact. Research, prototyping, writing and product design remain close enough that ideas do not disappear between departments.

The goal is not to appear larger. It is to move with clarity, publish with honesty and build systems that earn their complexity.
