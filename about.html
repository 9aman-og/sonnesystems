Sonne Systems is an independent AI research and engineering company founded by Aman Agarwal. We choose focused questions, build the experiment, and keep the uncomfortable result.

What we are building toward

AI that can adapt without losing its footing.

Our work connects brain-inspired computing with careful machine learning. We study sparse events, local adaptation, predictive state, structural growth, and the cost of computation.

The long-term question is simple to say and hard to answer: how can an intelligent system keep learning while staying efficient, testable, and honest about uncertainty?
