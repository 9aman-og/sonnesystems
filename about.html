About

A one-person studio, working in the open.

Sonne Systems is the research studio of Aman Agarwal: neuromorphic engineering and brain-computer interfaces, from the algorithm down to the silicon it should run on.

Principle · 01

Report what the experiments show

Our first paper's headline effect did not survive its own controls, and we published exactly that. Honest negatives beat pretty positives.

Principle · 02

Compute should follow difficulty

Brains spend energy where the signal is. We build models with the same manners: sparse, adaptive, and quiet when nothing is happening.

Principle · 03

Small tools, freely shared

Useful things we build along the way are left open for anyone, private by default, with no accounts and no cloud.
