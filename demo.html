    How it works

    Hybrid pipeline & efficiency

    1. 01

      MobileNet v3 · small

      A compact convolutional backbone reads the image once and hands up a feature vector.

    2. 02

      ca-LIF spiking head

      A leaky integrate-and-fire layer whose leak β and threshold ϑ adapt to each image.

    3. 03

      Breed by spike count

      Evidence accumulates over time; the class that spikes most, and soonest, wins.
